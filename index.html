System Requirements

  • OS X 10.11.6 or later
  • Intel® Core™2 Duo Processor (Intel® Core™ i5 processor or faster recommended)
  • 4 GB RAM (8 GB or more recommended)
  • 1280x800 display resolution
  • Core Audio compliant audio interface recommended
  • USB port (USB 2.0 or better) for program installation
  • Access to an internet connection for authorizing Live (for downloading additional content and updating Live, a fast internet connection is recommended)
  • Approximately 3 GB disk space on the system drive for the basic installation (8 GB free disk space recommended)
  • Up to 76 GB disk space for additionally available sound content

What comes with the download version?

  • A Live 10 installer to download from Your Account
  • A PDF manual included with your installer
  • The same included sounds as the boxed version. Sounds and Packs can be downloaded directly from the Packs section in the Live 10 browser by clicking the download icon, then clicking Install. They can also be downloaded from the web in the Your Packs section after login.

What extras do I get if I buy a box?

  • A USB Stick with your Live 10 installer and included Packs

Upgrade offer terms and conditions

  • Offer is not valid for not-for-resale versions (NFRs), educational licenses and multi-licenses.
